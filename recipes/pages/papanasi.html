Baked Papanasi (The Romanian Cheese Danish)

If you love cheese danishes but hate making a separate filling, you have to try this recipe—the cheese is mixed right into the dough!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups full-fat cottage cheese (drained)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • Sour cream & jam (for serving)
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix cottage cheese, eggs, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add baking soda and flour. Mix until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  4. Form dough into 8 rings or balls (flour hands lightly).
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with sour cream and jam.
